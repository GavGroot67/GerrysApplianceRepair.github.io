Appliance repair involves the diagnosis and repair of malfunctioning household appliances, such as: - Refrigerators - Ovens - Dishwashers - Washing machines - Dryers - Microwaves. Technicians use various tools and techniques to identify and fix issues, including: - Replacing worn or damaged parts - Repairing electrical circuits - Cleaning or replacing filters - Adjusting settings and calibration. The goal is to get your appliances working efficiently and safely, saving you time and money!
